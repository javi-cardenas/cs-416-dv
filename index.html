A Data-Driven Story of Modern Software Development

Stack Overflow Developer Survey 2024

Overview

This interactive visualization follows a slideshow narrative structure to guide you through a focused story about modern software development trends, gathered from the 2024 Stack Overflow Developer Survey. This webpage was created primarily using D3.js.

What You'll Discover

  • Programming Language Popularity: Explore the most widely adopted languages among developers worldwide
  • Compensation Trends: Understand how experience and language choice impact developer earnings
  • AI Tool Adoption: Investigate how developers are embracing AI tools in their workflow
  • Language-Specific AI Patterns: Discover which programming communities are most enthusiastic about AI adoption

The Data

This analysis draws from responses from over 65,000 developers worldwide, technology preferences, compensation, and AI tool adoption.
